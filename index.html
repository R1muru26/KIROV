⚙ Mechanics & Features

⚡ Power-Ups

Power-ups spawn randomly during gameplay. Fly into them to activate!

  • 🛡 Shield — Absorbs one hit. When active, you're immune to pipe collisions. Once the shield absorbs a hit, it breaks and you briefly blink/flash (a short grace period) before becoming fully vulnerable again. The blinking only happens AFTER the shield is consumed, not while it's active.
  • 🐢 Slow-Motion — Temporarily reduces pipe scroll speed by 40% for 5 seconds, giving you more reaction time.
  • ✕2 Score Multiplier — All points earned are doubled for 6 seconds. Stack with combo for big scores!
  • 🔮 Mini-Size — Temporarily shrinks the ghost's hitbox and sprite to 60% size for 5 seconds, making gaps easier to navigate.

🔥 Combo Streak

  • Consecutive clean pipe passes build a combo multiplier.
  • Combo resets on collision or game over.
  • Higher combo = more points per pipe cleared!

🚧 Special Obstacles

  • After 20 points, some pipes move up and down!
  • Stay alert — not all pipes move, keeping you on your toes.

🎨 Other Features

  • 🌙 Day/Night Mode: Toggle from the start screen. Background changes to match.
  • 🖼 Custom Skin: Upload your own avatar — it auto-resizes to fit the ghost shape.
